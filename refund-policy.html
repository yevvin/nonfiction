Refund policy

We have a 14-day return policy, which means you have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return.

To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it, unused, sealed, and in its original packaging.

To start a return, you can contact us at help@nonfiction-beauty.com To hasten the process, please provide us with your name, order number, product you wish to return, and reason for return. If your return is accepted, we’ll send you instructions on how and where to send your package. Items sent back to us without first requesting a return will not be accepted. You can always contact us for any return question at help@nonfiction-beauty.com

Damages and issues

Please inspect your order upon reception and contact us immediately if the item is defective, damaged or if you receive the wrong item, so that we can evaluate the issue and make it right.

Exceptions / non-returnable items

The followings are not eligible for returns/exchange

  • - Any items reported after 14 days from the date of receiving.
  • - Any items of which the sealing sticker has been removed, regardless of if they were defective on arrival.
  • - Any items with obvious signs of use or that has been opened.
  • - Any items not in its original condition, such as damaged or have missing parts, for reasons not due to our error.
  • - Any items returned without previous notice or in spite of return denial by our CS team will not be accepted.

Regarding return requests due to customer's change of mind, the return shipping fee is borne by customer, and the initial shipping fee deductions will be applied according to Nonfiction's return policy.

Exchanges

The fastest way to ensure you get what you want is to return the item you have, and once the return is accepted, make a separate purchase for the new item.

Shipping

Please kindly pay for return fee in advance as COD cannot be used for international shipping. The amount of the product will be refunded to the customers once parcels are arrived at our inventory and checked by the relative team.

Refunds

We will notify you once we’ve received and inspected your return, and let you know if the refund was approved or not. If approved, you’ll be automatically refunded on your original payment method. Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund too.

Duties and Taxes
